AI Ping Pong



Instructions

1. First, keep your laptop screen straight.
2. Press the play button.
3. Move yourself approximately 3-4 feet away from the laptop.
4. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending manner, and a red dot should appear on your wrist.
5. The red paddle is yours, it will move up and down depending on the position of your right wrist.
